Our Schools

Maria Margareth Schools

Educating for a Brighter Tomorrow

Education is the greatest equalizer. Through our network of Maria Margareth Schools, we are changing the lives of children who would otherwise be left behind including orphans, girls from low-income families, and children with special needs.

Our schools provide not just academic learning but also a nurturing environment where children are free to dream and reach their full potential.

Key Services Include:

  • A well-rounded curriculum aligned with national education standards
  • Life skills and values education
  • Nutritious meals and access to basic healthcare
  • Mentorship and psychosocial support
  • Affordable tuition and scholarship support for vulnerable students

When you support our schools, you're investing in the future of an entire community.
