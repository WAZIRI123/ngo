Who We Are

About Maria Margareth Foundation

Maria Margareth Foundation Tanzania is a legally registered nonprofit organization committed to creating sustainable change through education, healthcare, and community development initiatives. Since our establishment, we have been working tirelessly to empower vulnerable communities across Tanzania.

We serve both rural and urban communities by working closely with local leaders, stakeholders, and volunteers to co-create solutions that address root challenges. Our approach is driven by dignity, respect, and the pursuit of long-term impact.

Our Vision

"Quality education for a better life for everyone."

This vision inspires our work every day. To us, education is a powerful tool for equality, justice, and sustainable growth.

Our Mission

We aim to reach the poor, marginalized, and at-risk groups through community driven, voluntary self-help initiatives.

We don't offer handouts, we offer empowerment.

By increasing access to knowledge, resources, and opportunities, we enable people to lift themselves out of poverty.

Our Programs

Education & Skills Development

Providing opportunities for lifelong learning, from early childhood education to adult literacy and vocational training.

Community Health & Nutrition

Promoting access to essential health services, sexual and reproductive health education, and nutrition for vulnerable families.

Women & Youth Empowerment

Empowering women and young people through leadership programs, financial literacy workshops, and mentorship.
